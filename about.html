Ancestral Spices, Modern Soul

At Prime Sip Kitchen, our philosophy is anchored in honoring the culinary foundations of West Africa while presenting them on a global fine dining stage. We take pride in the meticulous preparation of traditional dishes: slow-cooking lamb shanks for six hours, drawing silk-smooth Ewedu infused with locust beans, and crafting our signature yaji blends by hand.

Our beverage program, the "Prime Sips", reimagines regional botanical staples like hibiscus, ginger, and tiger nut. Each drink is handcrafted daily to provide a clean, refreshing profile that balances the fiery richness of our pepper soups.

We invite you to sit back, toggle our custom ambient lounge audio player, and experience a sensory journey that celebrates premium ingredients, dynamic soundscapes, and outstanding hospitality.
